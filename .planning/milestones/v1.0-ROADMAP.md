## Overview

Complete inventory and sales management system for a single store with end-to-end visibility from point of sale through financial tracking to staff payroll. Includes secure authentication with role-based access control, atomic sales processing, real-time inventory management, financial reporting, and product lifecycle management with full audit trails.

**Goal:** Cashiers can process sales with real-time inventory visibility, and inventory decreases atomically with sale completion without data loss or race conditions.

- [x] 02-11: Fail closed on missing JWT_SECRET

**Delivered:**
- Atomic sales transactions with price snapshots from database (prevents manipulation)
- Row-level locking (SELECT FOR UPDATE) to prevent race conditions
- Product search by name or SKU with debouncing
- Store vs warehouse inventory tracking
- Inventory replenishment workflow with atomic transactions
- Cost field hidden from CASHIER role for RBAC enforcement

**Key Decisions:**
- Atomic transactions wrap sale creation, inventory decrement, and audit log in single block
- Price snapshots stored at sale time prevent margin calculation errors from product edits
- Separate warehouse and store inventory tracking prevents confusion
- JWT_SECRET now fails closed at startup if not set (production safety)
- Audit logging is atomic with the transaction it tracks

### Phase 4: Product Management CRUD Operations

**Goal:** Superadmin can edit and delete products with full audit trail. Product lifecycle is managed safely with historical data integrity.

**Plans:** 2 plans (all complete)
- [x] 04-01: Backend PATCH/DELETE endpoints with is_active field
- [x] 04-02: Frontend edit modal and delete confirmation

**Delivered:**
- Product editing with all field modifications
- Edit form shows before/after values for transparency
- Delta audit logging (only changed fields captured)
- Hard-delete protection when product has sales history
- Soft-delete fallback (is_active = false) for products with sales
- Delete confirmation dialog with sales count warning
- Full product snapshot logging for deletions
- Edit/Delete buttons on product table rows

**Key Decisions:**
- Products can be edited even with sales history (prices remain snapshots)
- Soft-delete (is_active=false) is offered when hard-delete is blocked
- Delta logging captures only changed fields to reduce log noise
- Full snapshot captured for deletions to support data recovery

## Milestone Summary

### Technology Stack Delivered

| Layer | Technology | Version | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|---------|---------|

| **Testing** | Vitest | 4.1 | Fast unit and integration tests |
| **Deployment** | Vercel | - | Configured via vercel.json |

### Key Accomplishments

1. **Secure Authentication System** — JWT-based login with role-based access control, HttpOnly refresh tokens, password hashing with bcryptjs
2. **Atomic Sales Processing** — Race-condition-free checkout with price snapshots, inventory validation, and transactional rollback
3. **Immutable Audit Trail** — Append-only logging of all state changes (user actions, sales, inventory, product edits) with no modification capability
4. **Role-Based Access Control** — Three distinct roles (Superadmin, Finance, Cashier) with endpoint-level enforcement and UI-aware navigation
5. **Product Lifecycle Management** — Edit/delete operations with historical data preservation, soft-delete fallback, and full audit logging
6. **Financial Reporting** — Sales dashboards with staff breakdown, margin calculation, reconciliation, and incentive tracking

### Architectural Decisions

| Decision | Rationale | Impact |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Immutable audit logging from Phase 1 | Cannot retrofit without data loss; compliance requirement | All 26 plans depend on this foundation |
| Atomic sales + inventory transactions | Prevents double-sells and inventory desync | Eliminated race condition vulnerabilities |
| Three-role RBAC (Superadmin/Finance/Cashier) | Balanced security and usability for small store operations | Clean separation of concerns |
| Separate warehouse/store inventory | Prevents confusion about available vs reserved stock | Simplified inventory logic |
| Manual incentive entry (no auto-calculation) | Business rules not yet defined; flexibility + transparency | Allows custom incentive strategies |
| Price snapshots at sale time | Edits don't affect past margin calculations | Historical data integrity guaranteed |
| Soft-delete fallback for products | Business continuity when deleting products with sales | Prevents data loss while enforcing audit |
